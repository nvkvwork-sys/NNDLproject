How it works and thresholds

This app uses a pre-trained DistilBERT model fine-tuned on SST-2 to output POSITIVE/NEGATIVE probabilities. A neutral label is inferred when the probabilities are close (near 50/50) or the confidence is below a threshold. You can adjust this threshold in app.js.
